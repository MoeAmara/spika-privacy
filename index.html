Spika Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Spika is a personal expense tracker. Your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a server that collects or stores your transactions.

Google Sign-In & Google Drive backup

If you enable backup, Spika uses Google Sign-In to access your own Google Drive. Backups are encrypted on your device before upload. We cannot read your backup contents.

Google Sign-In is used only for backup to your Google account — not for advertising or analytics.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not upload your transactions to our servers.
